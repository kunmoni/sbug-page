Features

Here are the cool things you get when you sign up and use Seatbug.

Smile high club

Connect with travelers across the world. Match by destination, hobbies, interests, languages, professions, and much more. Sit next to someone to practice a language, share tourist information, swap stories (or recipes!)

Become rich (sort of)

Offer your knowledge, share your expertise, and earn money while flying - set your hourly price and earn $20-$40! Or make use of your in-flight time by sitting next to an expert in something you want to learn about.

The ultimate social tool

We are social animals. Traveling is no different and it's great to meet new friends while at the airport lounge, in the queues, at the waiting areas,... and of course on the plane. Seatbug will connect travelers, and the world gets smaller (and better too)

More than flying

Overweight luggage? Save the hefty airlines extra fees (average $20/kg!) by sharing luggage allowance for a small fee. Need to work or just relax? Avoid chatty seatmates with a mutual quiet agreement. And enjoy the flight. On a budget? Save money by sharing airport-taxi expenses. That's another $30 saved! So many cool features and more coming soon!
